## Part II: China's AI Compliance Minefield

### The September 1, 2025 Red Line

If you're building AI products for the Chinese market, this date changed everything. **All AI-generated content must now be labeled.** Not suggested. **Mandated.**

**Key Regulations Timeline**:

| **Date** | **Regulation** | **Impact** |
|----------|----------------|------------|
| Aug 2023 | Generative AI Service Management | Algorithm filing required |
| Sept 2024 | AI Content Identification Rules | Watermarking standards set |
| **Sept 1, 2025** | **Enforcement begins** | **Fines up to $75K USD** |
| Q1 2026 | Cross-border data rules | Overseas API restrictions |

### The Compliance Checklist (Non-Negotiable)

**Must-Do**:
- ✅ Algorithm filing (if serving mainland users)
- ✅ AI content labeling (video/audio/text)
- ✅ User agreements updated (AI disclosure)
- ✅ Data localization (sensitive data stays in-country)

**Career-Ending Mistakes**:
- ❌ Using unfiled overseas APIs ($7.5K-$75K fines)
- ❌ Deepfakes without consent (criminal liability)
- ❌ Bypassing content moderation (platform ban + fines)
- ❌ Fake news generation (jail time)

### Platform-Specific Landmines
